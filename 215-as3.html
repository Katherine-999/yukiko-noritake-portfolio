About

Yukiko Noritake

Yukiko Noritake is a Paris-based artist. Originally from Japan, she studied French language and culture at university before following her true calling and fully dedicating herself to her artistic career. She later studied illustration in Paris and began her professional practice in 2018. The publication of several books by French publishers marked the start of her career.

She has developed a spontaneous approach to acrylic painting, enriched by expressive line work. Her visual world draws on a range of inspirations, including travel, interior spaces, both historical and contemporary art, as well as traditional craftsmanship from different cultures.
